What is eAdvocate?

eAdvocate is an online caring marketplace that helps people who need health and social services find helping professionals in their area.

As a dynamic caring community eAdvocate connects skilled caring professionals, from over 20+ professional disciplines, with individuals and families that need help and support. You can easily search, find and negotiate the care you need for you and your family today!

Why use eAdvocate?

Finding care is more accessible – you can search for multidisciplinary helping professionals in one place, negotiate what you need personally & confidentially

Where Can I find Caring Professionals?

eAdvocate allows you to browse the profiles of caring professionals available for work in your area. You are able to search for care and support based on your own personal needs, in areas such as palliative care, long-term care, acute health care needs or support for children with diverse needs. You can also post a personalized Request for Care outlining what you need and then choose from many skilled helping professionals who meet your requirements. eAdvocate allows you to be more in control of your health care and social service needs by negotiating and contracting directly with those helping professionals you feel are a fit.

How does Pricing & Payment work?

eAdvocate offers health care and social services that are more affordable and also provides helping professionals opportunities to be better compensated for their caring work. eAdvocate provides a safe, trusted and easy system to help negotiate all care and compensation between members.

eAdvocate holds all funds in escrow to ensure satisfaction and timley payments. eAdvocate deducts a small fee for the management of this caring marketplace.
